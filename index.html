A little about me

In a brief synopsis; my name is Chris Gramme and I love creating, problem solving and designing! I started building websites as a hobby at a young age, but mid-2010 is when I truly took flight into the vast skies of application development. My passion for creating has consequently lead me to pursue a very rewarding career in web construction and progression.


Shortly after purchasing my first smart phone I found myself immersed watching countless online tutorials with a goal of creating an Android application called "Car Maintenance and Fuel". Car Maintenance and Fuel was my first coding project and is the way I started teaching myself to program. After cramming my brain for thousands of hours with development practices and ideas I finally had a product that I am confident to say was one of the most rewarding undertakings I have yet encountered. When it comes down to it I love building things... I love the process of thinking my way through problems and overcoming what may or may not seem possible to overcome.

I knew a good amount of Android techniques, but desired to continue learning web development. This brought me to where I am today. I now have extensive knowledge of development skills with a variety of helpful resources to aid with creating and keeping up with current practices. As a self taught developer I can't help but accentuate my passion and experience with the mobile platform into my web applications. Two words sum it up--responsive design. I am a firm believer that every website must be optimized for any condition you throw its way. Although there are many sites floating the internet without the responsive bones they deserve, I can do my part to let us live on a planet that is plentiful with ems, percentages, meta tags, and media queries.
